CULTURAL EVENTS



Encore is one of the IET Lucknow cultural fest. Parmarth is the social club of IET Lucknow. The children of Parmarth performed a skit in Encore 2k17, which is the IET Lucknow Cultural Fest. This skit is related to one of the most important reasons, "How Parmarth works?". This was prepared by the volunteers of the Parmarth specially by the contribution of Ayush Agarwal &Antariksha.



SOCIAL SERVICES



Cloth distribution Event: It is organised twice every year, once in summer and other in winter. We collect cloth from people who have plenty of it, mostly from students, faculties and societies and distribute it to them who have scarcity of it, mostly slum dwellers and homeless people.
